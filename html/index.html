About Me

Bharat Singh

Yoga Instructor

Yoga means union — the connection of body, mind, and breath. For me, yoga is not just a practice, but a way of living with awareness, balance, and calm.

I believe yoga goes beyond physical movement. Through mindful poses, breathing techniques, and gentle guidance, I help people reduce stress, improve flexibility, and find mental clarity in daily life.

I offer online yoga sessions, making it easy for you to practice from the comfort of your home. These live and guided classes are designed to fit into your routine and are suitable for beginners as well as experienced practitioners.

With flexible timings and clear step-by-step instruction, online yoga allows you to stay consistent, connected, and focused—no matter where you are.

My aim is to help you reconnect with yourself, one breath and one pose at a time.
